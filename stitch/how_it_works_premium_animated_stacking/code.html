Common Inquiries

What is Stableford Scoring? expand_more

Stableford is a scoring system in golf which, rather than counting the total number of strokes taken, involves scoring points based on the number of strokes taken at each hole. This levels the playing field for players of all abilities.

How are winners selected? expand_more

The top 20% of scorers each month are entered into a digital grand draw, which is audited and live-streamed for transparency.
