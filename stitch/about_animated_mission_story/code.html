Why We Started

It began with a simple observation: the energy on the 18th hole is one of the most powerful forces in sport. We asked ourselves, "What if that energy didn't end at the clubhouse?"

Play With Purpose was founded to bridge the gap between high-performance lifestyle and community stewardship. We didn't want to create another charity tournament; we wanted to build a digital-first philanthropic engine that scales with the passion of its members.
